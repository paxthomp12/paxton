Giving Back

Youth Baseball Camp & Community Investment

Supporting STMA Youth Baseball | St. Michael, Minnesota

"Baseball has given me so much — lessons in discipline, resiliency, and teamwork that have shaped who I am today. Now, I want to give back by supporting the youth in my hometown through a baseball camp."

Through partnership with the Good Game Foundation, I'm investing in the next generation of players in my hometown. This camp is about more than drills and games — it's about encouragement, mentorship, and letting kids know that someone believes in them.

My goal is to be the example I wished I had growing up — someone who comes back to invest in the community and show young athletes what's possible. Together, we're building more than ballplayers. We're building confidence, community, and opportunity.
